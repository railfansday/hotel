List of largest hotels

This is a list of hotels that have 3,000 or more guest accommodation rooms. Since 2020, the largest hotel in the world is the First World Hotel in Malaysia with 6,119 rooms divided between three buildings. The largest single hotel building is the MGM Grand Las Vegas, with 5,124 rooms in one building.
